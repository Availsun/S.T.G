I am Sepideh Teimoory Garakani, born in 1987 in Tehran, Iran, and currently living in Netherlands.
I am a weaver of visual dreams, crafting stories in colors, lines, and beams. In the vast canvas of life, I find my muse, drawing inspiration from hues and views.
Life is a journey, filled with moments and emotions, and graphic design is my language, expressing the essence of this voyage. With each brushstroke, I compose a visual song, a melody of ideas where everyone belongs. Design is my bridge, connecting hearts and transcending barriers, conveying emotions through symbols and signs.
As life and design continually evolve, so too does my art, embracing endless possibilities. I am a graphic designer, an artist at heart, playing my part in the grand tapestry of life and leaving my mark with each creation.
Through the lens of design, I see the face of the world, capturing the essence of day and night, and inviting others to feel, reflect, and stay.
