In the fabric of life, art is the thread,
Weaving through moments where dreams are spread.
Design is the form, the structure, the line,
Guiding the chaos, making it fine.

Life is a canvas, broad and grand,
Each day a brushstroke, where our stories expand.
With colors and textures, both dark and light,
We paint our journey from morning to night.

Art is the soul, the heartbeat, the song,
A universal language where everyone belongs.
Design is the logic, the balance, the grace,
Bringing order and harmony, a true embrace.

Together they dance in an endless embrace,
Art and design, a warm interlace.
They shape our world, both seen and felt,
In every creation where emotion is dealt.
